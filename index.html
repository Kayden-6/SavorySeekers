Nizam's

Good restaurant for Kebab wraps and Halal too

Word Press

Nizam's in Dharmatala, Kolkata, is an iconic eatery known for its legendary Kathi rolls. Established in the early 20th century, Nizam's is credited with inventing the Kathi roll, a popular street food that consists of kebabs wrapped in paratha (a type of flatbread) along with onions, peppers, and various sauces.

Zakaria Street

The taste of community in the face of communal polarisation.

lifestyleasia

As the soulful sounds of azaan fill the Ramadan air, Kolkata’s Zakaria Street comes alive with a beeline of foodies from all over the city flocking to the narrow bylanes. The City of Joy lives up to its name as all religious differences crumble and Ramadan heralds a time for everyone to indulge in special delicacies prepared afresh at the roadside stalls here. From the juiciest kebabs to haleem, and hot milk drinks with sevaiyan (roasted vermicelli), here’s a complete guide to explore Zakaria Street — food to eat, restaurants to visit and a culture to imbibe.
